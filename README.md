Project Description: Our project addresses the way reporting and information are currently handled for the Pittsburgh Lajna (the women's side) of the Al-Nur Mosque. There are about 50 members, and the organization faces challenges due to information being scattered across multiple platforms. Key data such as attendance records, meeting notes, announcements, and reminders are spread between group chats, emails, online forms, and even paper documents. This lack of centralization makes it difficult to maintain a standard of consistency, ensure accuracy, and quickly access or share important information when needed to communicate with other members. Our solution to this problem was to create a one-stop shop website that can track all the information and data. It acts as a central hub that stores all the information in one place so it stays organized. It will improve accessibility and efficiency, and make managing activities, events, and information much easier.
